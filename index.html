About the Digital Innovation Summit

The Lesotho Digital Innovation Summit is an annual event organized by SkyAlpha HD in partnership with universities and institutions across Lesotho.

Our mission is to foster digital transformation in higher education by bringing together thought leaders, educators, and innovators to share knowledge and best practices.

Since 2020, we've hosted over 150 sessions with participation from all major universities in Lesotho, reaching thousands of students and faculty members.
