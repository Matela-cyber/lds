About the Digital Innovators Summit

Our Mission

Aligned with Lesotho's National Digital Transformation Strategy's Vision for 2030, the Digital Innovators Summit is more than an event - it's a movement toward creating a digitally empowered society.

By providing youth with critical digital skills, the summit strengthens Lesotho's workforce, fosters innovation, and enhances resilience in the face of misinformation and economic challenges.

Our Impact

  • Engaged 500+ participants from leading universities
  • 30% of attendees implemented digital tools post-summit
  • National recognition for advancing digital literacy
  • MOUs with universities for digital education continuity

Key Achievements

Broad Reach

Previous summits have engaged participants from NUL, Botho University, and Limkokwing University.

Advocacy

Participants have led campaigns against misinformation using AI tools introduced at the summit.

Collaborations

Government and private sector partners now support digital transformation policies influenced by summit themes.
