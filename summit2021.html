Digital Innovators Summit 2021

Introducing Digital Literacy and AI to Lesotho's Academic Community

View Gallery See Highlights

Summit Overview

The inaugural Digital Innovators Summit was held from June 15-17, 2021, marking the beginning of an annual tradition to advance digital literacy in Lesotho.

This first summit focused on introducing basic AI concepts and digital tools to Lesotho's academic community, with participation primarily from universities.

Key Themes
  • Introduction to AI
  • Digital Literacy Fundamentals
  • Academic Applications
  • Future Skills
